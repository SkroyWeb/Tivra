⭐ Course Introduction

Welcome to the One-Week Physics Orientation Program by Satyam Sir — a complete kickstart to Class 11 Physics designed to transform beginners into confident, concept-strong learners.

Class 11 Physics is the foundation of all advanced science—whether it’s JEE, NEET, CUET, or Board Exams. Most students struggle in the beginning because Physics suddenly becomes conceptual, mathematical, and application-based. This program is created to remove that fear—right from Day 1.

Across 7 high-energy sessions, students will understand how to study Physics the right way, how to approach numerical problems, how to break down difficult concepts, and how to build a strong base that supports them for the next 2 years.

Every session is designed to be interactive, practical, visual, and crystal clear, so students don’t just learn Physics—they experience it.

By the end of the orientation, students will know exactly what to study, how to study, and how to score high using proven techniques that toppers follow.

it’s your launchpad to becoming a Physics Champion.
